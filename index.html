Privacy Policy – Car Fusion

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

Developer: Sunil Kumar

Contact Email: sourcecodeforstartups@gmail.com

1. No Personal Data Collected

Car Fusion does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game is primarily offline and respects your privacy.

2. Limited Internet Use

This game works offline for gameplay. However, internet access is used only to display ads via third-party services like AdMob and to collect basic crash data through Firebase.

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses trusted services such as Google AdMob and Firebase for showing ads and collecting anonymous usage or crash data.

These services may collect non-personal information like device type, crash logs, or advertising ID as per their policies:

  • Google AdMob Policies
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

4. Local Data Storage

All gameplay data, such as scores or progress, is stored locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to the developer.

5. Children’s Privacy

Car Fusion is suitable for all age groups. It does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults.
